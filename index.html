About Me

I was formerly a Process Engineer at Micron, then left to pursue a Masters degree in IT Business (Analytics track) at Singapore Management University (SMU) to acquire technical skills in the data analytics field.

In my previous job, data analysis was the “bread and butter” of my core responsibilities to monitor, troubleshoot and optimize processes. During which, I learnt to visualize large volumes of data in ways that can tease out trends and patterns, then leverage on my domain knowledge to make connections and draw actionable conclusions. It was then I gained interest analytics, machine learning and the likes of it, and realised how valuable it was to gain data science skills in this digital era. This propelled me to pursue post-graduate education in analytics, during which I diligently took up numerous online courses from Udemy and DataCamp to supplement the learning from the masters programme at SMU.

Besides working and active learning, I participate in competitive women's football leagues and cups, and attend football trainings regularly as a committed club team member.
